What is it about?

The 18th edition of ICET conference will be held on 29-30 November 2022 at the National University of Computer and Engineering Sciences, Peshawar. In this modern era of Information technology. It is proposed to arrange an international conference annually by different institutions, under the umbrella of IEEE Islamabad section. All previous editions of the conference had their proceedings indexed and published by IEEE Xplore. Accepted papers will be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore's subject to meeting IEEE Xplore's scope and quality requirements.
